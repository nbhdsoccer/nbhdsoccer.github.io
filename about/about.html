Illinois Nonprofit Organization Building Stronger Communities

Chicago Neighborhood Soccer is an Illinois nonprofit organization founded in 2022 by experienced community leaders who have been organizing pickup games and leagues across Chicago's South and West Side neighborhoods for over a decade.

Our Nonprofit Mission

To cultivate inclusive communities through elevated and welcoming soccer experiences - one person and one event at a time.

Our Community Vision

To build meaningful connections that give our communities a sense of belonging while using soccer as a catalyst for positive neighborhood change.
