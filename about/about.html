The NBHD Soccer Story

How neighborhood pickup games became a community development nonprofit

2012

Four friends‚ÄîAndre, Diego, Julian, and Jordan‚Äîstarted organizing pickup games in their neighborhoods. No grand plan, just a love for soccer and a belief that sports bring people together.

2015-2020

Growing Through Word of Mouth

Our games grew organically. Neighbors brought neighbors. Word spread across communities. We learned that consistency, quality, and genuine welcome create lasting bonds.

2021

Recognizing Our Impact

With 6,500+ community members and 400+ weekly participants, we realized we weren't just organizing games‚Äîwe were building community infrastructure. It was time to formalize our work.

We incorporated as an Illinois nonprofit, bringing structure to our community development work while keeping our grassroots values. Our mission became clear: cultivate inclusive communities through soccer.

2023-2025

Comprehensive Community Development

Today we're partnering with CPS schools on youth programming, running memorial tournaments that fund community causes, supporting local artists, and maintaining accessible community soccer. We've proven that celebrating differences and focusing neighbors' energy creates lasting change across Chicago's diverse communities.
