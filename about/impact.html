Transparency & Accountability

Our commitment to openness about how community investments create neighborhood impact

Our Accountability Commitment

  • 100% Volunteer-Powered: No executive salaries, unpaid board of directors
  • Community Input: Major decisions discussed at open community meetings
  • Financial Openness: Reports available to community members and supporters
  • Impact Measurement: Regular updates with specific metrics and outcomes
  • External Validation: Partnerships with established organizations verify our work
  • Transparent Governance: Board decisions shared with community
