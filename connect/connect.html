A Personal Note

Hi, I'm one of the co-founders of Chicago Neighborhood Soccer. We started as neighbors organizing pickup games and grew into a formal nonprofit because we saw how sports could bring people together across Chicago's diverse communities.

Whether you're reading this as a school principal wondering if we're the right fit, a corporate partner exploring community investment, or someone who just loves soccer‚Äîknow that we approach every conversation with the same authenticity that got us here.

We're not a big organization with layers of bureaucracy. We're neighbors who figured out how to make community development work through soccer. That means when you reach out, you're talking to people who live in these communities and understand what works here.

- The NBHD Soccer Team
