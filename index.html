Your Neighborhood Programs

Community Pickup Soccer

Weekly games across our neighborhoods. We make pickup soccer findable, accessible, and welcoming for everyone in our community.

  • Tuesdays & Thursdays: Mixed pickup
  • Wednesdays: Women's only
  • Fridays: Community games
  • Saturdays: Beach soccer (seasonal)

REC 77: School Partnerships

In-house sports leagues co-developed with CPS schools. We bring professional coaching and equipment directly to students in our community.

  • 4-8 week customizable programs
  • Multiple sports options
  • Professional coaches & referees
  • Championship celebrations
Learn More

Youth Development

Partnering with YMCA, Chicago Park District, and Boys & Girls Club for youth tournaments and development programs in our neighborhoods.

  • 3-6 grade tournaments
  • 7-8 grade competitions
  • Alumni support programs
  • Coed opportunities

Community Building

Beyond soccer, we support local artists, fundraise for community causes, and strengthen neighborhood connections across the South & West sides.

  • Art Gala with $1000 artist grants
  • Memorial tournaments
  • Charity fundraising
  • Neighborhood celebrations
