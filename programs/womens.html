Why Women's Pickup Soccer is Hard to Find in Chicago

Most pickup soccer in Chicago is male-dominated. We created a different solution.

The Problem:

  • Male-dominated spaces: Most pickup soccer is 80-90% men
  • Intimidating environments: Fast, aggressive play that excludes many women
  • No consistent women's options: Sporadic or seasonal programs only
  • Skill level barriers: "You must be this good to play" mentality
  • Location gaps: Nothing focused on South & West Side communities

Our Solution:

  • Women-only space: 100% women every Wednesday
  • All skill levels welcome: From never-played to competitive
  • Consistent year-round: Rain or shine, every Wednesday
  • Community-focused: South & West Side neighborhood organizing
  • Affordable access: Just $10 per game, equipment provided
  • Safe environment: Led by women, for women

This is Community Infrastructure, Not Just Sports

As a nonprofit organization, we're building long-term community organizing capacity through sports. Women's Wednesday is part of our broader neighborhood development work.

Hello, Women's Wednesday!

Community Growth Announcement - February 2023

First and foremost

Thank you for coming out and kicking it with us. We are ecstatic about having you all join the Chicago Neighborhood Soccer community; this is for everyone! Everyone makes a difference, and the entire community is better for having you join it.

In these past 12 months, we have grown over 1,000 new members on meetup! Almost 20% of that has been women!

Special shout out to Community Leaders Shelby Ponder and Nicole Hack for working with us to make this happen

Building Something Sustainable

We plan on doing more for women's pickup soccer! To do that, we need to start charging enough to cover basic facility fees. We were happy to put a couple grand of our own money, and take that risk, to subsidize an opportunity for women to come out and play pickup soccer for the first two months.

Now that two months have passed, it's clear that this Women's Wednesday launch has been a great success!

We are excited to say Women's Pick-up soccer is here to stay.

Community-Driven Leadership

Our goal is to be community-driven. This means we want to include you, support you, and collaborate with you more.

Want to be an NBHD Community Leader? Everyone is invited to be a leader in our volunteer-powered community.

🏆 Tournament Planning in Progress

We're organizing Chicago's first dedicated women's pickup soccer tournament! This will showcase the incredible talent and community spirit of our Women's Wednesday players.

Tournament Goals:

  • Celebrate our community's growth
  • Competitive but supportive environment
  • Showcase women's soccer talent
  • Build connections across neighborhoods

What We're Planning:

  • Multiple skill/experience divisions
  • Team formation support
  • Awards and recognition
  • Community celebration event

This is Community Organizing in Action

Our tournament isn't just about competition - it's about building the kind of community infrastructure that makes neighborhoods stronger. When women have spaces to lead, organize, and celebrate together, entire communities benefit.
