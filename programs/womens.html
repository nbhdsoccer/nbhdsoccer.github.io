Community Growth Story

A message from our community leadership celebrating the success and evolution of Women's Wednesday

Hello, Women's Wednesday!

Community Growth Announcement - February 2023

First and foremost

Thank you for coming out and kicking it with us. We are ecstatic about having you all join the Chicago Neighborhood Soccer community; this is for everyone! Everyone makes a difference, and the entire community is better for having you join it.

In these past 12 months, we have grown over 1,000 new members on meetup! Almost 20% of that has been women!

Special shout out to Community Leaders Shelby Ponder and Nicole Hack for working with us to make this happen

Building Something Sustainable

We plan on doing more for women's pickup soccer! To do that, we need to start charging enough to cover basic facility fees. We were happy to put a couple grand of our own money, and take that risk, to subsidize an opportunity for women to come out and play pickup soccer for the first two months.

Now that two months have passed, it's clear that this Women's Wednesday launch has been a great success!

We are excited to say Women's Pick-up soccer is here to stay.

Community-Driven Leadership

Our goal is to be community-driven. This means we want to include you, support you, and collaborate with you more.

Want to be an NBHD Community Leader? Everyone is invited to be a leader in our volunteer-powered community.

Chicago Neighborhood Soccer Community Leadership Team

Women's Tournament 2025

We're excited to announce that we're planning our first annual Women's Tournament for 2025! This will be a celebration of our amazing community of women soccer players from across Chicago's neighborhoods.

The tournament will feature teams from our Women's Wednesday community and beyond, creating a supportive, competitive environment where women can showcase their skills and celebrate their love of the beautiful game.

What to Expect:

  • Teams from Women's Wednesday community
  • Supportive, competitive environment
  • Celebration of women's soccer excellence
  • Community building through sport
  • Awards and recognition ceremony

Stay tuned for tournament format, dates, registration details, and more information coming in the next few months!
